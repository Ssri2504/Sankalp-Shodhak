Our Experience

At Sankalp Shodhak, we are proud of our achievements in various competitive events. We have won many events, which include:

Pitch-O-Perfect Description

Pitch-O-Perfect is a prestigious college-level competition hosted by the Eureka event, organized by the E-Cell at IIT Bombay.

Our team proudly emerged as the winner at the college level, showcasing our innovative idea that captivated the jury.

We presented the Nyay Sahayak Application, a platform designed to connect individuals with experienced lawyers, streamline legal processes, and enhance access to justice.

The positive feedback from the jury affirmed our commitment to creating impactful solutions in the legal domain, making a significant mark in the entrepreneurial landscape.

These accomplishments reflect our commitment to innovation and excellence in our projects and initiatives.

SIH Internal Hackathon Description

SIH Internal Hackathon is a prestigious event that fosters innovation and problem-solving among participants.

At this hackathon, our team had the opportunity to present two groundbreaking projects: the Bail Reckoner and the Water Footprint application.

The Bail Reckoner is designed to assist undertrial prisoners, legal aid providers, and judicial authorities in streamlining the bail process, ensuring that justice is accessible and efficient.

Meanwhile, the Water Footprint application aims to raise awareness about water consumption by providing users with insights into the water used in various products and services.

Both projects received positive feedback from the judges, affirming our commitment to leveraging technology for social good and creating solutions that address critical societal challenges.

Solve the SDG Hackathon Description

Solve the SDG Hackathon organized by IIT Madras is a significant platform aimed at addressing the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) through innovative solutions.

Our team proudly participated in this competitive event and emerged as the runner-up.

During the hackathon, we focused on the critical topic of decarbonizing heavy vehicles, a pressing issue in the quest for sustainable transportation.

Our project aimed to develop a viable solution that reduces carbon emissions from heavy-duty vehicles, which are major contributors to air pollution and climate change.

By leveraging advanced technologies and innovative design principles, we sought to create a more environmentally friendly alternative for the transportation industry.

Our solution was well-received by the judges, highlighting our dedication to sustainability and the urgent need for effective strategies to combat climate change.

This recognition further motivates us to continue working towards impactful projects that align with the global sustainability agenda.
