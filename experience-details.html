Smart India Hackathon

The Smart India Hackathon was a nationwide initiative designed to provide students with a platform to tackle real-world problems. During this event, we collaborated with industry experts and mentors to develop innovative solutions addressing critical issues in our society. Our team presented the ideas of a Bail Reckoner and a Water Footprint, both of which were developed in accordance with government guidelines. The Bail Reckoner aims to streamline the bail process, making it more transparent and accessible to individuals in need of legal assistance. Meanwhile, the Water Footprint project focuses on raising awareness about water usage and conservation, helping communities understand their impact on this vital resource. Together, these projects exemplify our commitment to leveraging technology for social good.

Solve the SDG Hackathon

At the Solve the SDG Hackathon, we concentrated on developing innovative solutions aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. This event provided a unique opportunity to collaborate with passionate individuals who share a commitment to creating a positive impact on our planet. Our specific focus was on decarbonizing electric vehicles using DC-DC converters, which plays a crucial role in enhancing the sustainability of transportation. By leveraging our collective expertise, we aimed to develop effective strategies that reduce the carbon footprint of EVs and promote cleaner energy alternatives.
